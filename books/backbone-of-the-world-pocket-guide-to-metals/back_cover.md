Without metals, human civilization as we know it would cease to exist. From the iron in our blood to the lithium in our phones, these lustrous elements form the invisible skeleton of our reality. 'The Backbone of the World' takes you on a narrative-driven journey through the periodic table, exploring the atomic magic that makes metals strong, the fiery history of their extraction, and the high-tech future of the materials that shape our lives. Blending chemistry, geology, and history, this pocket guide reveals why the story of humanity is, fundamentally, the story of metals.
